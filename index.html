Project: AIS Dress Code

Our website serves as an information center for parents and students who are confused or unaware of the AIS uniform policy.

Making my homepage

 

Partners

  • Sara Al Rowaily
  • Jung Sub Lee

 

Client + Evidence

Mr. Hardisty is our client for this project. During our meeting, we discussed about the objectives for the website. He had lots of ideas including a simple interface that is userfriendly, lots of visual images, and it must look professional. If our website came out successful he said, he might include it in the actual school website.

 

Audience

For parents or students to check out a detailed school uniform/ free dress policy.

 

Objectives

  • For new students to have a clear understanding of the AIS dress code.
  • Clear Do.s and Don't.
  • Javascript for the dummy
  • One main focus, no blending of backgrounds
  • Userfriendly
  • Simple interface (little hyperlinks)
  • Looks professional (appropriate for all audiences eg. parents)

      WP5 QUESTIONS

      CONTENT

      • What message is your website giving out overall? Desribe your theme.

        • Our theme is baised on AIS, and simplistic which helps the student to recieve specific information.

      • What are essential elements to have on each page of your website?

        • Essential elements to have on each page of our website are headline, sub-headline, navigation and supporting image.

      • Will your page banner be on each page? If yes, is it in the same place? Is it the same size?

        • Yes, we will have a page banner on each page, and it will be in identical sizes and place.

      • What font will you use for titles or headings on your content pages?

        • Times New Roman is the font we used for our heading/title.

      • What color will you use for titles or headings on your content pages?

        • Black will be the colour used for out heading or title.

      • What font will you use for descriptions on your content pages?

        • Comic-sans was the font we used for the description of our content page.

      • What color will you use for descriptions?

        • Black will be the colour we use for the description of our content page.

       

      NAVIGATION

      • Where will you place the navigation bar on the page?

        • We will place our navigation bar on the top left side of our page.

      • What content will be on the navigation bar?

        • The content that will be placed on the navigation bar are our sub-pages.

      • Will the navigation bar be on every page?

        • Yes, the navigation bar will be on every page.

      • How will your navigation bar help visitors know which page they are on?

        • It hovers upon the page that has been clicked on, and it will have a subtitle.
